Here are some of the delicious proteins that we had while in the island paradise:


1. Sinugbang Anduhaw

We had some freshly grilled Anduhaw (island mackerel). The fish was bought in Palompon market just a couple of hours before going to Kalanggaman. I always love freshly grilled fish when going out to the beach. Grilled food goes so well with poso or hanging rice.

2. Grilled Pork

Pork hot off the grill is an instant hit for beachgoers. No leftovers are going to be expected when you have grilled pork. Sinugbang Baboy with a sawsawan made of vinegar, soy sauce, and chili peppers can turn anyone into a voracious carnivore.

3. Kinilaw

Mrs. Lequin prepared a special kinilaw (ceviche) made of flying fish, lukot (sea hare eggs), coconut milk, onion, ginger, and vinegar. The kinilaw was my favorite food that day. I have always been a big fan of the noodle-like lukot and I also love it in my fish soup. The kinilaw was well balanced, just the way I like it.
